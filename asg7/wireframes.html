1. Advantages and Disadvantages of Sketching Wireframes

Sketching wireframes allows designers to quickly brainstorm ideas and visualize layouts without worrying about technical precision. It encourages early decissions but also can lack accuracy. It may be hard to understand if you are not creating the wireframe yourself compared to digital tools which may be more clearer to understand.
