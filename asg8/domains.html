K-pop Budgeting Project: Domain Names

Discussion Summary

The 3 Possible domains for my website are: KpopCash.com, IdolBudget.com, and FandomFunds.com. We discussed that the domain name should be something straightforward and easy to understand. Something that was short but memorable was something I took into consideration

After considering this feedback, I decided on KpopCash.com .Its memorable, and clearly states the purpose of and directly conveys the purpose of the project. The name is easy to type, appealing to the target audience, and combines both themes of Kpop and Budgeting.
