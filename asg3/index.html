Problem

Kpop fans are constantly overspending on merch, concerts, new album releases etc. They stand in a lot of different groups and don't know how to manage/track their finances that they spend on these groups. The concept of â€œmoney comes back but the memories don't" is a term that a lot of fans (including myself) use to justify these purchases. While its fun to use this mindset, its not fun when you are 10k in credit card debt.

Audience

The primary audience for this project would be targeted to overspending K-pop fans (or just fans in general) who spend a lot on concerts, albums merchandise and other fan items.

Competition

Competition would be existing budgeting apps and tools and also possibly fan communities that talk about spending habits.

Key Messages

Fans can enjoy their favorite groups without financial stress. This website will help users track spending, budget for upcoming releases/concerts and help kpop fans make better purchase decisions. This website will help turn memorable moments into responsible spending habits.

Call to Action

When the target audience sees this app, they will feel encouraged to manage their fandom spending and think carefully before buying, and actively use the app to track and budget their K-pop expenses.
