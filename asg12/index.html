My Favorite Movies

Here are 12 of my favorite movies. I love these films because of the visuals, storyline or just actors that play in it.

White Chicks

This movie never fails to make me laugh. While it may have some inappropriate jokes, the overall movie is hilarious.

Turning Red

I love the animation of this movie. It's heart-felt and always makes me smile.

Train to Busan

Every time I watch this movie, I cry. Its really good and has some really interesting charachters.

My Neighbor Totoro

One of my favorite Studio Ghibli movies. Totoro is adorable and I love the nature elements in the movie.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

Though there are many different spiderman movies, this has to be my favorite one.

Maze Runner

Full of suspense, no matter how many times I rewatch, I am still on the edge of my seat.

Kpop Demon Hunter

I thought this movie would be cringe at first, but ended up enjoying it a lot. The OST really makes this movie stand out.

How to Train Your Dragon (Live Action)

The classic story brought to life in live action. Incredible adventure and breathtaking visuals.

20th Century Girl

A nostalgic romance with a twist at the end. The visuals and storytelling make this movie unforgettable.

Big Hero 6

Large fluffy, marshmellow looking charachter. Of course its my favorite. I LOVE BAYMAX .

Coraline

This movie scared me as a kid, but regardless is still one of my favorites.

Spirited Away

My second favorite Studio Ghibli Movie. The food in the movie always makes me hungry.
