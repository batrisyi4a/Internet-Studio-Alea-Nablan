Explore Kuala Lumpur

1. Visit the Batu Caves

Climb the vibrant rainbow staircase to the Batu Caves, a sacred Hindu site surrounded by limestone hills. It’s a breathtaking combination of nature and spirituality.

2. Walk Through Central Market

Discover local handicrafts, batik art, and souvenirs at Central Market — a cultural hub where traditional art meets modern Malaysian creativity.

3. Relax at KLCC Park

Enjoy a peaceful stroll by the lake at KLCC Park, surrounded by lush greenery with the Petronas Towers gleaming in the background.
