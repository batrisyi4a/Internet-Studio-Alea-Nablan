Design Statement

I chose Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia as my dream vacation destination because I enjoy visiting everytime I go. Despite going often, I never tend to get bored because there is just so much to visit and explore.

For typography, I paired Poppins for headings and Open Sans for body text. These two fonts go well together and showcase the Malaysian culture well.

I chose these images to showcase the colorful aspect of Malaysia. Malaysia known for its melting pot of cultures from Chinese, Indian and Malaysia, I think the mix of colors was a great way to emphasis this.
