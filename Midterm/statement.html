Design Statement

I chose Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia as my dream vacation destination because of its rich blend of culture, tradition, and tropical vibrancy. The city’s mix of modern skyscrapers and cultural landmarks inspired a warm and lively color palette with golden oranges and lush greens to represent energy and nature.

For typography, I paired Poppins for headings and Open Sans for body text. These fonts complement each other — Poppins gives a clean, confident look, while Open Sans offers readability and friendliness. I placed text with generous spacing and centered main sections to maintain balance and focus on imagery.

Images were chosen to highlight Kuala Lumpur’s cultural energy — local food, traditional sites, and city views. The buttons are rounded and bright to create inviting calls to action. Their placement below content and in the hero section draws visitors’ attention toward exploring and engaging with the site.
