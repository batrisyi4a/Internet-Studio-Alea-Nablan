Places To Eat

1. Aunty Jenny

Though the drink is not culturally Malaysian, Aunty Jenny has some of the best Boba Teas you can get in Malaysia. The floral teas really stand out and its unique toppings are definently worth a try as well.

2. Chicken Rice Shop

Chicken rice is what they do best! While it may sound simple, chicken rice is a complex dish that is packed with flavor and a must try in Malaysia

3. Madam Kwan

Madam Kwan serves an abundance of traditional Malaysian food. From Nasi Lemak, Chendol, Kwey Taw, Madam Kwan will make you feel like you entered your grandma's kitche
