Places To Eat

1. Warung Mamak

Experience authentic Malaysian street food — from roti canai to teh tarik. This open-air warung captures the heart of KL’s food culture.

2. Nasi Lemak House

Enjoy Malaysia’s national dish — fragrant coconut rice served with sambal, crispy anchovies, peanuts, and egg — all wrapped in banana leaves.

3. SkyGarden Café

Dine with a skyline view! SkyGarden offers fusion Malaysian cuisine on a rooftop with breathtaking night views of the city lights.
